# Gas laws
$$\frac{P_1V_1}{T_1}=\text{constant}=\frac{P_2V_2}{T_2}$$
$P$ is absolute pressure
$V$ is air volume
$T$ is absolute temperature (in kelvin)
and there is alternative equation 
$$\frac{P_1}{T_1\rho_1}=\text{constant}=\frac{P_2}{T_2\rho_2}$$
$\rho$ is air density
