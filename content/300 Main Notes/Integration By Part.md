# Integration By Part
It came from product rule of [[Derivatives]]

$$\int f(x) g'(x)dx = f(x)g(x) - \int g(x)f'(x)dx$$

$$\int u dv = uv - \int v du$$
(where u = f(x) and v = g(x))
