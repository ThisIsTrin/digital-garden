# Indefinite Integral
$$\int f(x) \, dx = F(x) +c$$
## Properties
$$\int cf(x) \, dx = c \int f(x) \, dx$$
$$\int f(x) \pm g(x) \, dx = \int f(x) \, dx \pm \int f(x) \, dx $$
## Formula (Memorize)
$$\int 1 \, dx = \int dx = x+c$$
$$\int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} +c \, , (n \ne -1)$$
$$\int x^{-1} \, dx = \int \frac{1}{x} \, dx = \ln(x) +c$$
$$\int e^x \, dx =  e^x +c$$
$$\int \cos x \, dx = \sin x +c$$
$$\int \sin x \, dx = -\cos x +c$$
