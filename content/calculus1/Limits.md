$$ \lim_{x\rightarrow a} f(x)$$
# One sided limit
Right handed limit
$$ \lim_{x\rightarrow a^+} f(x)$$
Left handed limit
$$ \lim_{x\rightarrow a^-} f(x)$$
Both right-handed & left-handed limit has to exist & equal to each other for normal limit to exits ( $\lim_{x\rightarrow a} f(x)$) 
# Continuity
$$ \lim_{x\rightarrow a} f(x) = f(a)$$
